About Us

"Lost In Space" is a dynamic startup with a unique mission dedicated to nurturing young scientific talent and fostering a global perspective among children, transcending geographical boundaries. We offer transformative international experiential learning programs that encompass science, technology, art, and culture. Our pioneering achievement lies in being the first startup globally to facilitate satellite launches in collaboration with high school and college students. This accomplishment underscores our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what young minds can achieve. At "Lost In Space," our unwavering commitment revolves around identifying and nurturing young talents in the fields of science and technology. We provide a global platform for these budding geniuses, enhanced by meaningful interactions with prominent Indian scientists who serve as both inspirations and mentors. Our flagship initiative, the "Young Scientist Program," is designed to ignite scientific curiosity among high school students, deepen their understanding of science, and guide them towards promising careers in research. However, our mission extends beyond traditional workshops. We've introduced an innovative approach that empowers students to create DIY models of small satellites, including balloon satellites and suborbital satellites. These models are assembled during school workshops and further developed in the comfort of students' homes. Once these DIY satellites are complete, they are sent back to us. Subsequently, we collaborate with esteemed organizations such as ISRO to launch these satellites into space. The data collected from these satellites serves as a wellspring of inspiration and enthusiasm for the students, motivating them to dive deeper into the realms of science and space exploration. "Lost In Space" is not just a startup; it's a movement aimed at transforming young minds into curious and capable scientists. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and exploration while also fostering a sense of unity and collaboration that transcends borders. Through our innovative programs and initiatives, we aim to inspire the next generation of global scientific leaders, breaking down barriers and paving the way for a brighter future.
